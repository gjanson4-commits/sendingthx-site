The problem

You sent it. Did they get it?

E-commerce is almost 30 years old, and we're still checking delivery status like it's 1996. You spent time finding the perfect gift. And then — silence. Did it arrive? Did it get damaged? Do they even know it was from you? There's still no immediate way to say thank you.

The tracking page says delivered. But you still don't know.
The fix

One text. One Thx.

One normalized delivery event powers the whole loop — from the moment an order ships to the moment a thank-you lands.

🎁

Gifter sends a gift

Orders from their favorite shop. Enters a message. Hopes for the best.

📦

Carrier marks delivered

Package is on the doorstep. Tracking page updates. Gifter refreshes. Still waiting.

⚡

SendingThx fires

Recipient gets a branded text. And can send a thank-you in seconds. Gifter's phone lights up. Loop closed.
